About Us

VishTek Group based in Sharjah, UAE, is a specialized trading and technical solutions provider serving the Oil & Gas, Power Generation, and Desalination sectors across the Middle East and South Asia. Founded by an industry veteran with over 17 years of hands-on experience, VishTek is built on deep domain knowledge and a commitment to engineering excellence. We offer fit-for-purpose products, reliable logistics, and customized technical support for EPC contractors, utilities, and industrial end users. With active representation in Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Oman, and India, we deliver globally sourced solutions with regional responsiveness, ensuring compliance, quality, and performance on every project.

Vision

To be the partner of choice for high-performance electrical, instrumentation, and industrial solutions — driving progress in critical infrastructure through precision sourcing, technical depth, and a customer-first approach.

Mission

To deliver reliable, high-quality products and tailored solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients. We achieve this through a steadfast commitment to engineering standards, service excellence, and continuous innovation.

Our Core Values

We operate with honesty, accountability, and full transparency in every transaction; ensure reliability through consistent, on-time delivery of quality E&I materials; foster long-term partnerships built on trust and responsiveness; pursue excellence by upholding high standards in all our products and services; and embrace sustainability through responsible decisions that support long-term industry and environmental goals.
